I am Parth Wadhwa, the designer behind ChemicalDzns, building punchy graphic systems for teams, creators, tournaments, and brands.

The work leans into bold contrast, electric blue energy, clean type, and sports-led composition. The goal is simple: visuals that hit fast and stay memorable.
